# www.W9YT.org 
Source for the Badger Amateur Radio Society's website for resources available 
to students, faculty, and community members. The Badger Amateur Radio 
Society (W9YT) is a registered student organization at the University of 
Wisconsin - Madison, furthering the art of amateur radio for any and all 
interested parties.

## About BARS
W9YT BARS is a historic amateur radio club located on the University of 
Wisconsin - Madison campus, under the College of Engineering's Department of 
Electrical Engineering. The purposes of the club are to promote local amateur 
radio activity within the club and within the area, to provide the opportunity 
to learn about and interact with amateur radio as a hobby, to provide club 
members the opportunity to practice good operating skills and test equipment, 
and to provide a recreational connection to scientific concepts UW-Madison 
students and faculty interact with on a regular basis.
